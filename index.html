Projects

AUTOMATED TRANSPORTATION VEHICLE

The task of the AUTOMATED TRANSPORTATION VEHICLE is to correctly identify, pick-up and deliver the goods that were specified by the respective manufacturing sector of the industry. The transceiver module which comprises of Wi-Fi, GSM technologies helps in data communication between the vehicle and the Instructor. With various sensor and GPS technologies the vehicle travels through the industry with the pre-defined path that was fed into its memory. With the help of camera the goods are perceived and processed with the help of Image Processing technology, for the matching of the specified ones. Thus the specified goods are transported and delivered.

CAR ANALYSER

To predict a car for an user based upon his actual requirements rather than all the car promotions that are broadcasted. To categorise the vehicles based upon economy ,luxury , performance with regards to their built materials. To get input from the users in order to decide the best fit. We will use machine learning algorithms inorder to get the requirements from the user. For e.g., the environment in which the user uses the vehicle, miles travelled per day, performance requirements etc. Finally , the analysis will yield a result which will be a right choice for the user based upon the simple information given rather than more complex technical terms.
